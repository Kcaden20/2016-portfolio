About Me

I am a Colombian-American Graphic Designer currently based in Rhode Island. I enjoy primarily branding, web design and UI/UX. In my personal work, I'm exploring more experimental realms of the browser such as with this as well as the balance between structure and order as with this. In my free time, I practice Capoeira which is referred to as "dance fighting" to my chagrin.

I have worked with design agencies such as Linchpin, new and promising student groups such as Hack@Brown and established groups like A Better World by Design. I am currently working with the MIT Mobile Experience Lab and am Co-Director of RISD QUICKIES. Find me on Twitter, Instagram and LinkedIn.
